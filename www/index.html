Welcome to the official site of the AHP Baseball Academy

Our Mission

  1. To help teenage boys develop into young men of integrity and empathy who will lead, be responsible, and make the world a better place.
  2. To help our players not only reach the next level (college or professional baseball) but have success once they get there.

ABSOLUTE HUMAN PERFORMANCE

Was founded in 2017 and first and foremost is:

An athletic performance centre:
serving to train any athlete of any sport looking to maximize his or her potential, as well as any person who may be simply looking to make a commitment to improving his or her fitness and health.

AHP has many programs under its umbrella:
this elite baseball academy, on-ice hockey skills training, and as it grows will continue to offer more sport skill services, however its roots will always be in strength and conditioning; in the training of athletes.

The AHP Advantages

Unmatched Individualized Programming

Our professional coaching staff will help you execute your plan with precision. We have coaches with experience coaching in college and high school, as well as playing at the NJCAA, NAIA, NCAA DII, NCAA DI, and Professional levels

Cutting-Edge Technology

Utilize the latest technology to improve and recover. Rapsodo, Motus, Blast Motion, Marc Pro

Full College Placement Service

We will help you find the right fit. All In-House. Absolute Human Performance has placed 27 players since 2019. Taylor Burns (Program Director) has placed 64 players since 2013. See our list of alumni to see for yourself.

World-Class Facility

Practice in ABSOLUTE HUMAN PERFORMANCE 3.0, our world-class turf indoor training facility in St. Albert, AB.
