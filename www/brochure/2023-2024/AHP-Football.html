AHP FOOTBALL PERFORMANCE

This program is for the serious football athlete only, who is looking to play at the next level. Our mission is simple: to not only move our players on to junior, college and/or professional football, but to prepare them to succeed once they get there.

We strive to leave no stone unturned in providing an integrated, holistic approach to developing the football athlete.

INDIVIDUALIZED SKILL DEVELOPMENT

The AHP Program is unique in that we will put what is best for each individual player’s development first. This may mean maximizing skill work, or it may mean prioritizing the weight room and/or speed development more – as just one example. Each athlete will receive a highly detailed and intensive plan tailored to their specific needs and long-term plan.

  • Individualized Position-Specific Development
  • Combine Preparation & Training
  • Situational Awareness/Reads Development
  • Individualized Power-Speed-Strength Development
  • Tactical and Mental Game Coaching
  • Game Applicable Drills
  • Nutrition Coaching & Guidance

RECRUITING & EXPOSURE

We believe in taking ownership of the recruiting process. We offer guidance and mentorship on both the football and academic side.

We like to tell our athletes it’s their responsibility to develop their game, and it’s ours to get their video in front of as many college coaches as possible.

STRENGTH & CONDITIONING

AHP is incredibly unique in our approach to training.

It all starts with a thorough assessment process. From there, every aspect of the training program is individualized: warm-ups, lifts, speed and agility work, and mobility training.

Additionally, athletes will be coached on nutrition strategies and have access to cutting-edge recovery modalities such as MarcPro, and Gladiator.

STATE OF THE ART FACILITY

Absolute Human Performance is a 16,000 square foot facility located in St. Albert, in Campbell Business Park.

It houses a full fieldhouse and weight room.

Staff

Calvin McCarty

Head Coach

Calvin is the Head Athletic Performance Coach at AHP.

Calvin played fourteen seasons in the CFL (thirteen for the Edmonton Elks). He is one of just four running backs in the league’s history to play 200 games. He is a Grey Cup Champion (2015), and won Canadian Player of the Year twice, as well as CFLPA All-Star twice.
Prior to his professional career, Calvin played college football at Western Washington University and Boise State. Calvin brings a wealth of coaching experience to the table.

Additionally, he’s always been heavily involved in community service: winning the David Boone Award, and nominated twice for the Tom Pate Award.

Taylor Burns

Performance Coordinator

Taylor is the Founder, President, and Director of Athletic Performance at Absolute Human Performance.
Taylor, a Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) through the National Strength & Conditioning Association has worked with athletes at all levels: from youth sports to the professional ranks.

A former college pitcher (at Hutchinson College and the University of Central Missouri) Taylor’s shoulder injury led him into the field of strength and conditioning. While he has a wide knowledge base, he specializes in program design, maximum strength and power development, post-rehab, and athletic performance.
