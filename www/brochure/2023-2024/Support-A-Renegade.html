About the academy

The AHP Baseball Academy started in 2021.

In just a short time the program has helped 15 players move on to the collegiate baseball level in the United States.

Over the past decade, the coaches of our program have helped 70 Canadian baseball players play collegiately south of the border.

The AHP Academy provides an incredible experience and environment for its players to pursue their academic and athletic goals. The programâ€™s mission is two-fold and simple:

  1. To help teenage boys develop into young men of integrity and empathy who will lead, be responsible, and make the world a better place.
  2. To help our players not only reach the next level (college or professional baseball) but have success once they get there.

Learn more about how we can help YOU through our player sponsorship opportunities.

The Support A Renegade Program, is simply that: believing and supporting a High School baseball player in their desire and dream of playing collegiate and potentially professional baseball. We are looking for community partners who are interested in helping players be able to participate in a program that will allow them to reach their goals. Your commitment can significantly impact their experiences and future.
