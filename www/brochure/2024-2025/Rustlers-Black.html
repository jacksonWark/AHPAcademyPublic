AHP Rustlers Black BASEBALL ACADEMY

This program is for the serious baseball athlete only, who is looking to play at the next level. Our mission is simple: to not only move our players on to college and/or professional baseball, but to prepare them to succeed once they get there

FALL & SPRING COMPETITIVE SCHEDULE

  • 20 Game Fall Schedule
  • 20 Game Travel Spring Schedule (B.C, Okotoks, etc.)
  • 1 Week Long Spring USA Showcase Tournament
    • Likely will be in May 2025

INDIVIDUALIZED SKILL DEVELOPMENT

The AHP Academy is completely unique in that we will put what is best for each individual player’s development first. This may mean logging many innings or at bats for the fall, or it may mean prioritizing the weight room in the early off-season – as just one example. Each athlete will receive a highly detailed and intensive plan tailored to their specific needs and long-term plan.

  • Customized Throwing Programs
  • Customized Hitting Programs
  • Edgertronic Camera for Video Analysis for Mechanical Efficiency
  • Trackman for Pitch Design and Hitting Feedback
  • Rapsodo Hitting for Instant Feedback
  • Weekly Mental Game Coaching Sessions
  • Nutrition Coaching & Plan

COLLEGE PLACEMENT

We take complete ownership of the college recruiting process. This means there’s no need for any outside recruiting service – we do all of that work for you.
We like to tell our athletes it’s their responsibility to develop their game, and it’s ours to get their video in front of as many college coaches as possible.

Our track record of helping our players receive scholarships speaks for itself

WORLD CLASS ATHLETIC PERFORMANCE TRAINING

AHP is incredibly unique in our approach to training.
It all starts with a thorough assessment process. From there, every aspect of the training program is individualized – from the athlete’s warm-ups to their lifts, to their arm care routine, to their speed and agility training.
Additionally, athletes will be coached on nutrition strategies and have access to cutting-edge recovery modalities like MarcPro.

STATE OF THE ART FACILITIES

The Academy trains at Absolute Human Performance: a 16,000 square foot, state of the art training facility – one of the best in all of Canada
